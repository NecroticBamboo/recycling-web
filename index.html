London waste

Climate change and increased pollution due to badly controlled waste production is becoming more and more important nowadays. London as a huge capital city produces wast amount of domestic and industrial waste every day. This pile of garbage needs to be handled and handled fast and a new batch coming the next day.
Over the years London has built an infrastructure allowing it to handle its waste. According to the sources its rapidly reaching its capacity. London needs more. So, not only London officials and businesses, but we all need to play our part in waste reduction.

London also needs to up its game on resource efficiency – we still produce too much waste and don’t recycle enough, and we don’t treat water like the scarce resource it is.
Sadiq Aman Khan, Mayor of London
