Why to recycle?

Every day people are getting richer and richer. We buy a lot of things without knowing how it affects our environment. Now world faces global warming we need to think about preserving our planet. Recycling is one the best ways to help the worlrd that you live in. It makes a huge impact on the environment and humans in general. Without our help lots of species of animals and plants can go instinct. It is our duty to protect the environment and all living creatures.

What to recycle?

With the use of new technologies we can recycle almost all things that we use with some exceptions. For example, things like plastic bottles, food and paper can be easily remade into something new without harming the environment. Some things such as clothes, batteries and plastic bags cannot be recycled. For more information please visite the Utilisation tab.

Where to recycle?

Collection

Separate rubbish collection is something that was with us for years. Rubbish separation takes a key role in recycling chain. Currently most of London collects plastic, glass and metal for reusing. Sometimes (mostly in residential areas) compostable food waste and garden waste collected using separate containers.

Recycling

The problem is that various London borrows have different collection policies. Recycling availability depends on contract borrow have with the recycling facility. It’s up to us to raise the demand for more types of materials to be recycled. It’s absolutely unacceptable that some types of plastic that could be recycled goes straight to landfills.
