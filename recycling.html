Where to recycle?

Collection

Separate rubbish collection is something that was with us for years. Rubbish separation takes a key role in recycling chain. Currently most of London collects plastic, glass and metal for reusing. Sometimes (mostly in residential areas) compostable food waste and garden waste collected using separate containers.

Recycling

The problem is that various London borrows have different collection policies. Recycling availability depends on contract borrow have with the recycling facility. It’s up to us to raise the demand for more types of materials to be recycled. It’s absolutely unacceptable that some types of plastic that could be recycled goes straight to landfills.
