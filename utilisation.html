Waste utilisation

Rubbish we can’t reuse or recycle needs to be utilized somehow. Here we are talking of enormous amounts. Tons and tons of rubbish produced every day. London has two types of general waste utilization - burn and landfill. The company behind it is Cory Riverside Energy. They collect rubbish using waste trucks and barges. All this litter then goes to an incinerator plant near Erith and a landfill on the other side of the river.

Take a look at that table made by Department for Environment Food and Rural Affairs. This table shows the proportion of collected household waste which is recycled or composted from 1998 to 2019. It only shows one region of London and England as a whole. The numbers are calculated using this formula:

X/Y*100 where:
  • X = Tonnage of household waste collected by the Waste Collection Authorities(WCA) which is sent for recycling/composting
